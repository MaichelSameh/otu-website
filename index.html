the October University of Technology

October Technological University is an Egyptian government university, one of the technological universities that was established in accordance with Law No. 72 of 2019 regarding the establishment of technological universities in Egypt. They are educational institutions that follow the method of education and training for students in various specializations needed by the labor market while developing and building the necessary skills to enroll the graduate in the labor market. Directly, the university is located in 6th of October City, Giza Governorate, on an area of 49 thousand square meters. The university includes the Education Development Fund building, the university administration building, 2 classroom buildings, and the workshops and laboratories building. The duration of study at the university is four years, and the university awards academic degrees according to The specialization is an above-average professional diploma in technology, a professional bachelor’s degree in technology, a professional master’s degree in technology, and a professional doctorate in technology.
