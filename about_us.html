Why choose October Technological University

In accordance with Law No. 72 of 2019, technological universities were established to achieve several goals, the most important of which is: creating a new integrated path for applied and technological education and training, parallel to the path of academic education. Applying technology and exploiting it for the benefit of society and qualifying graduates to meet the needs of the labor market. Providing technological education that provides high-quality educational and training services. Continuous development of curricula and study plans for all stages and levels of study. Providing technical assistance and administrative advice in the field of technical education and training.
